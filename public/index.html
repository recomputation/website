recomputation.org Home Page

Jul 1st, 2013

Welcome to recomputation.org. It is a repository for experiments in computational science.

We are very happy to announce that we now have 7 recomputable experiments from papers presented at CP 2013. Find them here.

Welcome to authors of accepted papers from the Constraint Programming conference CP 2013. We are delighted to be giving tutorial on recomputation there. A bit more on our plans for CP 2013 is in a recent blog post. We especially thank Christian Schulte and Laurent Michel for their help.

The Recomputation Manifesto describes the motivations behind the site.

We have now released our first experiment. We hope to grow the number of experiments soon.
