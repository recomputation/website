Welcome to recomputation.org. It is a repository for experiments in computational science.

We are looking forward to the ECAI 2014 tutorial on recomputation , taking place on August 19, 2014. We will soon be uploading new machines contributed by authors of papers from that conference.

Last year we gave a tutorial tutorial on recomputation at the Constraint Programming conference CP 2013.

We now have 7 recomputable experiments from papers presented at CP 2013. Find them here.

The Recomputation Manifesto describes the motivations behind the site.

We have now released our first experiment. We hope to grow the number of experiments soon.
